      2. Every day, when we go to work, we hope to do five simple things:

    • Share stories with delicious cups of coffee with our friends, customers.

    • Make anyone have a wonderful experience with the best flavor, mixing with deep, light music. We always hope that we can become a place, where you can come anytime and share stories with us.

    In 2014, Our owner, Mr. Stephan, an executive IT engineer, started traveling to Asian. He wanted to visit Vietnam a long time ago. He came Vietnam in 2014. He walked into a coffee store, he was impressed by views and quality of drinks. They decorated specially, combined with traditional and modern space. Moreover, they selled with reasonable price, only $2 per cup, music was really deep, light and remarkable, lovely space from outside to inside.

    After coming back Geneva, Switzerland, he wanted to open his business with Vietnamese coffeehouse tradition. In order to attract more clients and create new experiences, he combined Vietnamese coffee and modern European space. Customers really like this space and he has received so many positive feedbacks.
